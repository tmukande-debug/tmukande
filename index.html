About

My research focus areas are in Graph Neural Networks, Transformers, Recommender Systems and LLMs!

Currently pursuing a PhD in Machine Learning at SFI ML-Labs, Dublin City University, under the guidance of Dr. Annalina Caputo, Dr. Ruihai Dong (UCD) and Prof Noel O'Connor. My academic journey began with a bachelor's degree in Computer Software Engineering from Northeastern University, China. My research interests revolve around Natural Language Processing, particularly in applying Pre-trained Large Language Models including vision-language models for Sentiment Analysis and Question Answering related topics. In addition to my studies, I have gained valuable research experience working as a research intern at Huawei Noah's Ark Lab and IBM Research-China. During my research career, I have been fortunate to work with Dr. Jennifer Foster, Dr. Yvette Graham, Prof. Qun Liu, Dr. Lifeng Shang, and Dr. Xin Jiang.
